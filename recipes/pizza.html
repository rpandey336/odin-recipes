PIZZA

Description

Momo is very popular Nepali dish which is native to Newari community of Nepal.Everyone love momo and i also love momo so much

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Chicken Kimma
  • Chopped Onions
  • Spices and salt

Steps

  1. Make momo wrapper using wheat flour
  2. Mix chicken kimma ,onion and spices together
  3. Wrap prepared kimma with momo wrapper
  4. Steam cook the prepared momo balls in steamer for about 15 minutes
